www.reverbnation.com/jazzentertainer

Could you give me a call when you get a chance or could we set up a phone call?

For the last 15 years, The David Patrone Ensemble has been one of San Diego's most highly acclaimed and sought after acts, performing Jazz Standards and other tunes from the American Songbook. In 2009 David Patrone was the first vocalist ever to receive a San Diego Music Awards in the category of Jazz. Nostalgic while offering something new, David is an incredibly engaging and entertaining musician who interacts with the audience and provides one of San Diego's most impressive displays of talent and musicianship.

Thank you for your time.
